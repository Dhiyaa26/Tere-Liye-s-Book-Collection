About Tere Liye

Full Name: Darwis

Date of Birth: May 21, 1979

Biography and Career: Tere Liye is the pen name of Darwis, a well-known Indonesian author whose works have captivated readers across the country. Born on May 21, 1979, in Lahat, South Sumatra, Indonesia, Darwis adopted the pseudonym "Tere Liye" which means "for you" in Hindi, symbolizing his deep connection to his readers. He grew up in a simple family, which shaped his humble and reflective nature. Tere Liye pursued a degree in Economics at the University of Indonesia, but his passion for writing eventually took him down the path of a successful literary career. His novels have touched on a wide range of topics, from love and family to social issues and personal growth, often carrying deep moral messages
